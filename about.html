Our Story

Growing into a household name in Marin County & the Bay Area.

We are a local brand and clothing company representing Marin County, CA. We started this journey in Summer 2023 — and since then, Made in Marin has been growing into a brand that is becoming well known in Marin County and the Bay Area!

We are PROUD to say that we are building a COMMUNITY of brand supporters and SHOP LOCAL enthusiasts!

We use social media to show you the best of Marin County — and all of the local spots we love to support. Shouting out small businesses in Marin County and the North Bay Area is one of our favorite ways to give back!

Who We Are

Born & Raised in Marin

Made in Marin is a small business owned and operated by a husband and wife who were born and raised in Marin County. They live in Novato, CA — and the business, like many other small startups, is run out of their home.

The Made in Marin trademarked logo is sure to be seen all over the community!
